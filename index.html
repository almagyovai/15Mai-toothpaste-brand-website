No deforestation

Traditional toothpaste contains palm oil, which we replaced with coconut oil a more sustainable and efficient source of oil that does not contribute to deforestation.

Marine life

The colourants in traditional toothpastes are toxic to our marine life, so we got rid of them.

No more plastic

Our product is 100% biodegradable, including the package, which breaks down in only 5-10 years, not turning into microplastics.

Cruelty free

The plastic in a toothpaste takes 500 years to fully degrade. Us getting rid of it from our formula makes it cruelty free and safe for the environment.
